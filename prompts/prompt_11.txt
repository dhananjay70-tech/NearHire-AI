# PROJECT NAME

NearHire AI
AI Career Coach + Hyperlocal Job Intelligence Platform

=========================================
PROJECT GOAL
=========================================

Transform the existing NearHire project into a production-quality AI Career Coach that helps users throughout their career journey.

The platform must not only search jobs but also act as a personal AI mentor.

The backend should continuously collect real job listings from public company career pages and supported public job sources, normalize them, and store them in PostgreSQL.

The frontend should feel like a premium SaaS platform similar to Linear, Vercel, Notion, Stripe, and Lovable.

Do NOT create a basic CRUD application.

Build a scalable product.
